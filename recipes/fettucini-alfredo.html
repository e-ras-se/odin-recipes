Fettucini Alfredo

Description

Fettuccine Alfredo is a pasta dish consisting of fettuccine tossed with butter and Parmesan cheese which is melted and emulsified to form a rich cheese sauce coating the pasta.

Ingredients

  • Pasta:
  • Butter
  • Cream
  • Seasonings
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a rolling boil. Cook fettuccine at a boil until tender yet firm to the bite, about 8 minutes. Drain.
  3. Heat butter and cream in a large saucepan over low heat until butter melted; add garlic salt, salt, and black pepper.
  4. ncrease the heat to medium; stir in Romano and Parmesan cheeses until melted and sauce has thickened.
  5. Add cooked pasta to sauce; toss until thoroughly coated. Serve immediately.
